About Me

My name is Nikki. I am 31 years old, I grew up in Orem, Utah, and currently live in Saratoga Springs, Utah. I have been married to my husband, Alex, for 8 years. We have 3 mini pigs; Lilly, Booker, and Piper that are our babies.

I love educating people on mini pigs, especially because there is a lot of misinformation out there. I run a website called www.pigipedia.org, that is a wiki you can search for general information, as well as specific health conditions on mini pigs. I also run a Facebook page of the same name, where you can ask questions to knowleable admins as well as current mini pig owners.

In my spare time, I love to play video games. My current obession is a game called Heroes of the Storm.
